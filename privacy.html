Privacy Policy

Lunaris uses Google Analytics to collect basic usage data, such as page views, device/browser information, approximate region, and interaction events. This helps understand how the site is used and improve the experience.

Lunaris may store settings, bookmarks, search history, and browser data locally in your browser using localStorage, cookies, caches, service workers, and IndexedDB. You can export, import, or wipe this local data from Settings.

Do not enter sensitive personal information unless you understand where it is being sent. Third-party websites loaded through Lunaris may have their own privacy practices.
